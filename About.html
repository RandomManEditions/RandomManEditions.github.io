RANDOM MAN publishes limited edition art objects in Brooklyn, NY.

The publications promote fringe, overlooked, uncommon works and encourage conversation between unlikely artists working with a variety of media and concepts. RANDOM MAN serves as an archive for the underrepresented.

The objects come together as a collaboration between artist, publisher and friends. They have taken the form of VHS tape and book, but will take unforeseen forms in the future.
